   6 Common Medications That May Lower Your Dementia Risk

   5 min read
   [108]Dana G. Smith

   Dana G. Smith

   One exciting — and surprising — development in dementia prevention in
   recent years is the shingles vaccine. A number of studies have
   suggested that it lowers the risk of dementia by about 20 percent.
   [109]Dana G. Smith

   Dana G. Smith

   There’s often a question of correlation versus causation with this type
   of research. But the way the shingles vaccine was rolled out gives
   scientists confidence that the vaccine itself is offering some
   protection.
   [110]Dana G. Smith

   Dana G. Smith

   On the flip side, many studies have shown that antihistamines are
   associated with an increased risk of the disease. They work by blocking
   the activity of a neurochemical that is important for attention and
   memory.
   [111]Dana G. Smith

   Dana G. Smith

   In total, I looked into 10 common drugs that have been tied to
   dementia. For some medications, there may actually be something
   biological going on to cause the association.
   [112]Dana G. Smith

   Dana G. Smith

   With others, it may just be a correlation: The connection could be due
   to other health-related behaviors, underlying medical conditions or
   just incomplete data.
