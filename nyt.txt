   underway.

   [61]Luke Broadwater

   Emergency Operations Center, or PEOC, which was built during World War
   II to protect President Franklin D. Roosevelt.
   [62]Luke Broadwater

   Luke Broadwater

   but some information has emerged, including that the underground
   complex would have a bomb shelter and a full hospital.

   construction and annual maintenance.
